Sharing Guidebook

This handbook is a compilation of sharing patterns in leading biomedical research studies. The main question is this: Do studies approach sharing data and results similarly? Are there are a couple similar patterns? Or, is each study unique and artisanal in its methods and approach to sharing?

Equal Access

Equal access is a model of governance where research participants and research scientists share individual-level research data with each other. Researchers do not have unilateral control over data generated in the study. Raw data flows in both directions. If you sign-up for study practicing equal access, then you should expect to be able to access your slice of data in the study. [more...why/when important?]
